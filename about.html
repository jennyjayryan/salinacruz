Our Story

About Salinacruz

From a single Chihuahua in Hamilton to New Zealand’s most decorated smooth coat kennel — the Salinacruz story spans more than 35 years of passion, dedication, and an uncompromising love of the breed.

Yvonne Tester — Salinacruz Kennels
How It Began

One Chihuahua from a Neighbour

It started simply — a Chihuahua acquired from a neighbour in Hamilton. That one little dog set in motion a lifelong journey that would make Yvonne Tester one of New Zealand’s most respected Chihuahua breeders.

Yvonne established Salinacruz Kennels in 1989, eventually settling in Tauranga where the kennel has been based ever since. What began as a personal fascination with the smooth coat Chihuahua grew into a dedicated breeding programme built on careful study of the standard, selective use of bloodlines, and an uncompromising commitment to producing the best dogs possible.

The early years involved learning the breed from the ground up — studying pedigrees, travelling to shows, seeking out the right dogs to build the foundation of the kennel. That foundation would prove exceptional.

“You have to love the breed first and the winning second. If you get the dog right, the winning takes care of itself.”

Yvonne Tester — Salinacruz Kennels

Over three and a half decades, Salinacruz has produced more than 35 New Zealand champions. Dogs bred here have gone on to win in Australia, America, and South Africa. And in the greatest achievement in New Zealand Chihuahua history, Salinacruz bred Jacko — New Zealand’s first and only Grand Champion Chihuahua.

Our Approach

Breeding Philosophy

Every decision at Salinacruz is guided by the same principles that have shaped the kennel since 1989.

🏆

Type Above All

Correct Chihuahua type is non-negotiable. Every breeding decision begins with the standard — the apple dome, the characteristic expression, the proud carriage. Pretty wins in the short term; correct type builds a legacy.

📈

Selective Breeding

Salinacruz produces a small number of litters each year. This is deliberate. Fewer litters mean more attention to each one, more careful selection of breeding pairs, and more time to properly evaluate and place every puppy.

🌎

The Best Bloodlines

When the right dog doesn’t exist locally, Salinacruz looks further afield. Imports from Italy and the United States have brought fresh genetics and new dimensions of quality to the breeding programme — complementing, never replacing, the kennel’s own proven lines.

💌

Movement & Structure

A Chihuahua should move as well as it stands. Sound structure, correct angulation, and effortless movement are evaluated in every breeding animal and every potential keeper. A dog that moves well is a healthy dog.

❤️

Temperament Matters

The Chihuahua is a big personality in a small package. Salinacruz dogs are confident, alert, and affectionate — the qualities that make them exceptional show dogs and even better companions. Temperament is never an afterthought.

🏠

Raised With Care

Every Salinacruz puppy is raised in the home, handled from birth, and socialised to be confident in the world. Puppies are placed only with families who have been carefully considered and who share our commitment to the breed.

Key Milestones

35+ Years of History

1989
Salinacruz Kennels Established
Yvonne Tester registers the Salinacruz prefix in Tauranga, New Zealand, beginning what would become one of the country’s most successful Chihuahua breeding programmes.
Early 1990s
First Champions
The kennel produces its first homebred New Zealand champions, establishing Salinacruz as a serious force in New Zealand Chihuahua showing.
2000s
International Exports Begin
Salinacruz dogs begin making their mark overseas, with exports to Australia, the United States, and South Africa going on to achieve championship titles in their adopted countries.
2010s
Jacko — NZ’s First Grand Champion
Salinacruz breeds Jacko, who becomes New Zealand’s first and only Grand Champion Chihuahua — a milestone in New Zealand show dog history.
2020s
Italian Imports Strengthen the Programme
Eddie and Otto arrive from Italy, bringing exceptional continental bloodlines. Eddie earns Best in Show at a Specialty; Otto holds titles in both America and New Zealand.
Today
35+ NZ Champions & Still Going
With nine dogs in the current show team and more than 35 champions to the kennel’s name, Salinacruz remains at the forefront of New Zealand Chihuahua breeding.

The Breeder

Meet Yvonne Tester

Yvonne Tester has devoted more than 35 years to the smooth coat Chihuahua. Based in Tauranga, she is widely regarded as one of New Zealand’s most knowledgeable and accomplished Chihuahua breeders — a reputation built not on self-promotion but on the quality of dogs that carry the Salinacruz name.

An experienced championship show judge, Yvonne brings a deep understanding of the breed standard to every evaluation she makes — whether in the ring as a judge, or at home when deciding which puppies to keep. That same critical eye has guided every breeding decision at Salinacruz for over three decades.

Yvonne is passionate about sharing her knowledge of the breed and is always happy to talk Chihuahuas with people who share her love of these remarkable little dogs.
