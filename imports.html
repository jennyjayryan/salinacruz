Our Imported Dogs

Every import has been carefully selected to complement and strengthen the Salinacruz breeding programme.

🐶
🇮🇹
🌐 Imported from Italy “Eddie”

NZ Ch Misty Meadow’s Boston-PC

BISS NZ Champion Imp Italy

Best in Show Specialist and New Zealand Champion. Eddie arrived from Italy bringing outstanding European structure and type that has proven highly influential in our breeding programme.

🐶
🇮🇹
🌐 Imported from Italy “Otto”

AM Ch & NZ Ch Pasticciotto-PC Di San Gimignano

AM Champion NZ Champion Imp Italy

A dual American and New Zealand Champion imported from Italy. Otto is a dog of exceptional distinction, having achieved championship status in two countries and bringing elite Italian bloodlines to New Zealand.

🐶
🇺🇸
🌐 Imported from USA

AM GCH & CAN Ch & NZ Ch White O Morns Aztec Legend

AM Grand Champion CAN Champion NZ Champion Imp USA

A tri-country champion of the highest order — American Grand Champion, Canadian Champion, and New Zealand Champion. Aztec Legend represents some of the finest American show bloodlines available.

🐶
🇺🇸
🌐 Imported from USA

AM Ch & CAN Ch, NZ Ch & AUST Ch Daggot’s Well-Cut Tanzanite

AM Champion CAN Champion NZ Champion AUST Champion Imp USA

An extraordinary four-country champion — American, Canadian, New Zealand, and Australian titles. Tanzanite is one of the most titled imports Salinacruz has brought to New Zealand, with championships spanning three continents.

🐶
🇯🇵
🌐 Imported from Japan

Bramver’s Brahman

Imp Japan

Imported from Japan, where Chihuahua breeding is held to an exceptionally high standard. Brahman contributes unique Eastern bloodlines rarely seen in New Zealand, adding valuable diversity to the Salinacruz gene pool.

🐶
🇱🇻
🌐 Imported from Latvia

NZ Ch Lanas Legacy Orlando

NZ Champion Imp Latvia

A New Zealand Champion sourced from Latvia, tapping into the strong Eastern European Chihuahua breeding tradition. Orlando has proven his worth both in the show ring and as a breeding asset at Salinacruz.

🐶
🇫🇷
🌐 Imported from France

Mayerling of Alixora Blue

Imp France

Sourced from France, bringing the refined European style that French breeders are renowned for. Mayerling of Alixora Blue adds a distinctive French influence to the Salinacruz bloodlines, broadening the international reach of our programme.
