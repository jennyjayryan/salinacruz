Our Story

Passionate About the Chihuahua Since 1989

From a single Chihuahua acquired from a neighbour in Hamilton to over 53 New Zealand champions — Salinacruz Kennels has been shaping the breed in this country for more than three decades.

Founded by Yvonne Tester in 1989, Salinacruz has built an international reputation for producing long and smooth coat Chihuahuas of exceptional structure, movement, and showmanship. Our dogs have become champions in New Zealand, Australia, America, and South Africa.
