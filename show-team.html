In the Ring

Current Show Team

Nine exceptional Chihuahuas competing at the highest level across New Zealand. A mix of homebred champions and carefully selected imports, united by outstanding type and ring presence.

The Salinacruz show team is the result of more than 35 years of selective breeding, importing the world’s best bloodlines, and an uncompromising commitment to the long and smooth coat Chihuahua standard. Every dog in the team has been chosen for correct structure, breed type, and the character that makes a Chihuahua truly great in the ring.

Home of Jacko

Salinacruz is the breeder of Jacko — New Zealand’s first and only Grand Champion Chihuahua. A milestone in New Zealand show dog history, Jacko’s achievement stands as the highest individual honour in New Zealand Chihuahua showing and represents the pinnacle of what the Salinacruz breeding programme has produced.
